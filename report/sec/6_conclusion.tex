\section{Conclusion}
In this paper, we proposed a MARL gating framework designed to enforce computational budget constraints and aggregate global context, thereby improving the efficiency and representational capability of Linear Attention Vision Transformers. Our model achieved preliminary accuracies of $48.88\%$ and $58.37\%$ on the ImageNet100 and CIFAR-10 datasets, respectively. While the proposed framework remains in its early stages of development, it demonstrates interpretable saliency maps and yields a measurable accuracy improvement over the existing RALA\cite{fan2025breaking} softmax ranking baseline. Future work will focus on refining the reward optimization process, expanding our empirical evaluation to a broader suite of baseline architectures and higher-resolution configurations, and conducting comprehensive ablation studies to deeply analyze the framework's internal dynamics.
